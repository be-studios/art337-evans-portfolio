Interactive Packaging

This interactive packaging for a ream of handmade, recycled paper communicates to it's audience the importance of making sustainable paper choices. The package starts by displaying the distance between the Earth, Moon, and Venus. As the user opens the package, the package extends to reveal that the stacked height of the amount of trees used in one year for paper is closer to Venus than the Moon is. The back of the package reveals the breakdown of data. Sources can be found by scanning the QR code on the front. To create this mockup, graphics were designed in Adobe Illustrator and then mounted onto bristol board. All surfaces of the package can be viewed in the PDFs below.
