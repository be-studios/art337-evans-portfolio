Generative Sound

This is a generative sound that can play for an extensive amount of time without ever repeating. It was created in metasounds using scale to note arrays. Square, sine, and saw oscillators are multiplied and crossfaded with an oscillating crossfade value. This sound is mixed with square and triangle oscillators which are also multiplied and crossfaded. These are put through a ladder filter and the structure is duplicated. The duplicated structures are then mixed together to add a layer of depth.
