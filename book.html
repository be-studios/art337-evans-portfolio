Book Cover

The typography for this book cover was created using a fusion of digital tools and concrete objects. Type was crafted by hand and then photographed and digitally manipulated in Adobe Photoshop. The first set of type is formed from a combination of crushed glass and mustard seed. The mustard seed symbolizes the agricultural aspect of the book while the crushed glass represents the characters' break from the lab. For the word NIHH, paper was cut and a shadow was cast. This shadow represents the looming presence of the organization that haunts the plot.
