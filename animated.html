Animated Poster

This animated poster warns of the dangerous incline in the Bornean Orangutan population due to deforestation. The icons were hand-drawn and then vectorized in Adobe Illustrator. Premiere Pro was used to animate both the icons and typography. This piece won the best concept award for animation in the Juried Student Exhibition at Illinois State University's University Galleries in 2020.
