Installation Design

The Joe McCauley Gallery at Heartland Community College presented My Covid Year: a selection of artwork from diverse perspectives of what COVID brought them. The following pieces are graphics designed for the community response wall and for promotion of the show. A large banner was created for the wall as well as sheets with quotes from the artists. The audience then wrote their responses to the show on sticky notes and placed them onto the wall.
